Advancing the Future of Digital Risk

The Institute of Digital Risk (IDR) prepares the next generation of professionals to understand, manage and govern digital and cyber risk in an increasingly complex technological world. Through a unique combination of academic insight and industry collaboration, IDR equips practitioners with the skills required to address emerging digital threats and opportunities.

About IDR

The Institute of Digital Risk (IDR) is an industry-led training and deployment institute focused on preparing professionals for roles in digital, cyber and technology risk. The organisation works closely with partners across academia, industry and government to develop practical capabilities that address the challenges of modern digital infrastructure.

Operating in highly regulated and high-consequence environments such as financial services, healthcare and critical infrastructure, IDR provides structured training programs and applied research opportunities. Through collaborations with UK universities and global industry experts, the institute bridges academic knowledge with real-world projects to produce highly capable digital risk practitioners.

Service Pillars

Academy

IDR’s academy provides specialised training programs and bootcamps designed for students and professionals who want to build careers in digital risk, cybersecurity and technology governance. The curriculum blends theory with real-world case studies and practical exercises.

Innovation & Incubation

Through research initiatives and collaborative projects, IDR develops innovative digital risk frameworks and AI governance models. The innovation lab supports experimentation with emerging technologies while ensuring responsible and ethical deployment.

Advisory Services

IDR supports organisations in designing and implementing robust digital risk frameworks. The advisory team assists businesses in aligning with international standards such as NIST, ISO 27001 and NIS2 while strengthening governance and compliance capabilities.

Community

The IDR community brings together students, early-career professionals and experienced practitioners who share a commitment to advancing digital risk capabilities. Members come from sectors including financial services, technology companies, government agencies and critical infrastructure providers.

Through workshops, collaborative projects and networking opportunities, the institute fosters a global community of professionals dedicated to strengthening digital resilience and promoting responsible technology governance.
